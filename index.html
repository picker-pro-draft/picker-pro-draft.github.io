Picker.pro

Pick the right products or ideas by applying your criteria.

What is it?

Picker.pro is a game changer in product comparisons.

Comparisons which work for you

You know what's important to you - Picker.pro will help you select products based on your criteria

Science-based approach

The Must-Should-Could-Won't technique is scientifically proven productivity life hack for fast and conscious decisions

Great visualization

Picker.pro will order and highlight products that you like based on how well they meet your criteria

Shared decisions

You can invite friends or partners to help you find more options, figure out your criteria and advise!

No rush

You can spend a many days gathering your options and assigning the criteria - your session will be securely preserved

Valuable insights

Picker.pro will discretely advise you on what criteria others applied when picking a similar type of product, and what products where considered by other people with similar criteria

How does it work?

Next time when you have a serious decision to make...

First think about what matters to you...

Group your criteria into 4 categories - Must, Should, Could, Won't.

This will help you look at the products from the point of view of what really matters to you.

  • The criteria are yours - can be absolute or very subjective (e.g. 'pretty' or 'respected brand')
  • The less important a criteria is to you, the lower its priority - yes, it's that simple!
  • You can add, change or remove criteria at any time.

Thinking and visualizing your criteria helps you make more concious decisions and makes you more resistant against fooling ads!

...then start gathering your options...

Add the products you find interesting to your list in Picker.pro

  • This will be your collection of bookmarks
  • Picker.pro will try to extract the most relevant product info from the shop based on your criteria
  • Picker.pro will also suggest you the products that might be interested for you based on your criteria and based on what others selected!
  • Gain real-life insights on what is actually good, rather than well advertised!

...and then assign the criteria to products!

Drag and drop the criteria that you created to the products that meet them.

  • Picker.pro can order your list based on how well the criteria are met.
  • You will easily see which products are best for your needs.
  • You can also add your extra notes to each product

You've built your list of products which allows you to make an informed decision on what best for you!

The visual representation of the options and criteria they meet will improve your decision making!

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a look at some of the typical questions about Picker.pro - but feel free to drop us a line if you have any other questions!

  • Just another product comparer?

    No - the typical product comparers allow you to either:

    • Compare only the price of the same product in various shops
    • Compare the features of products only within a single shop
    • Compare specific products against the criteria that they find important

    Picker.pro allows you to compare products from various shops and judge them based on your own criteria.

  • Do I have to pay?

    No - the free version gives you the same user experience - you have access to full capabilities of Picker.pro.
    The paid versions are there to allow you some extra functionalities which are not absolutely necessary.
    They are also there to cover the costs of running this service, so don't hesitate:)

  • Does it exist already?

    No - we are just launching this project - we're gathering feedback from you to see whether the idea is hot or not :)
    If you like the idea, be sure to let us know - we're will only create the app if people are interested!

  • When is it going to be ready?

    We are currently gathering and measuring the reception around the world. Once we gather enough potential users, we will start the implementation. Follow us on social media or join our newsletter to stay up to date!

  • "Storing comparisons" - what does it mean?

    When you have a serious decision to make, such as buying a car or a fridge, you want to take your time. Spend a couple of days reading reviews, finding best deals and thinking about what you need. Storing a comparison means to save the options you've collected and the criteria you created and assigned, so that it does not disappear when you close the browser window.

  • "Multiple comparisons" - what does it mean?

    When you need to buy more than one thing, for example a new fridge and an oven and a vacuum cleaner - you'll be able to store this comparisons on separate pages - just as if you were using pen and paper to write them up.
